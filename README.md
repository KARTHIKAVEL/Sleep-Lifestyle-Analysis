# 🛌 Sleep & Lifestyle Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes how lifestyle factors such as stress, BMI, age, gender, and heart rate impact sleep quality and duration.
The analysis is performed using "Excel, SQL, and Power BI", and presented through an interactive dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Understand patterns in sleep duration and quality
* Identify how lifestyle factors influence sleep
* Analyze distribution of sleep disorders
* Provide insights for improving sleep health

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Excel → Data Cleaning
* SQL → Data Analysis & Querying
* Power BI → Dashboard & Data Visualization

📌 Key Insights

 🧠 Sleep & Stress

* Increased stress levels lead to reduced sleep quality
* Individuals with lower stress tend to have better sleep duration

 ⚖️ BMI Impact

* People with 'normal BMI' have higher average sleep duration (~7.4 hrs)
* 'Obese individuals' show lower sleep duration (~6.8 hrs)

👥 Gender Analysis

* Sleep duration is almost similar across genders
* Stress levels slightly vary between male and female groups

🎂 Age Factor

* Sleep duration gradually decreases with age
* Younger individuals tend to sleep longer

 😴 Sleep Disorders

* Majority of individuals have **no sleep disorder (~59%)**
* Remaining population affected by:

  * Insomnia
  * Sleep Apnea
